Gracie Abrams

Photo by Justin Higuchi on Flickr.com.

Gracie Abrams is a 25-year-old singer-songwriter from Los Angeles. Abrams is an indie-pop artist who gained attention from uploading music to SoundCloud and YouTube.

She rose to fame in 2020 when she released her debut EP, Minor. Minor explores themes of heartbreak and self-reflection, and it was praised for its intimate and emotional lyrics. She captures the complexities of relationships and personal growth, which resonates with listeners.

In an interview with Flaunt Magazine, she said, "I cannot imagine not feeling so intensely and deeply about everything. I used to turn to writing more immediately in extreme moments of pain. Now, I don't rely on writing as my tool for feeling better. I find myself leaning into writing about mundane details that spark my interest."

Since her EP, Abrams has released two albums and opened for artists like Olivia Rodrigo and Taylor Swift. She has also been nominated for Best New Artist at the 66th Annual Grammy Awards.
